Zhengbo Zou

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering and Engineering Mechanics at Columbia University. I am also an affiliated member of the Columbia University Data Science Insitute and the Zuckerman Mind Brain Behavior Institute.

I lead the Intelligent Construction Lab at Columbia ( ICON@Columbia ). We aim to understand how intelligent robots interact with human operators, co-workers, and the built environment. We leverage machine learning and control theory to design, build, and control these robots, and conduct human participant experiments to study their impacts on the end users.

Before joining Columbia, I was an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at UBC.
